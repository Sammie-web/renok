Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are RENOK rolling papers made from? +

RENOK rolling papers are crafted from premium, natural fibers, ensuring a clean and smooth burn. We offer varieties made from organic hemp, rice, and wood pulp, all sustainably sourced.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we do! RENOK proudly ships worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location. Please see our shipping policy for more details.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website's "Track Order" page or the courier's website to monitor your shipment's progress.

What is your return policy? +

We want you to be completely satisfied with your RENOK products. If you're not happy with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Please ensure items are unused and in their original packaging.
